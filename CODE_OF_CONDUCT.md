# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the VibeCrack community should be respectful, direct, and professional.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Being respectful in discussion and review
- Giving actionable technical feedback
- Assuming good faith while staying rigorous
- Keeping user safety and responsible disclosure in mind

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Publishing private vulnerability reports or private data
- Encouraging illegal or unauthorized use of the project
- Posting secrets, credentials, or customer information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or users that violate this policy.

For serious issues, contact `security@vibecrack.com`.
